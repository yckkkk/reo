## Practical UI constraints

- Controls that drive the same task stay close together: recording waveform, timer, stop, pause, cancel.
- Button hierarchy is single-primary per surface.
- Disabled buttons are avoided where validation-on-submit can provide clearer feedback.
- Form labels are visible above fields; placeholders are not labels.
- Icon-only controls require explicit accessible names and focus-visible tooltips.
- Sidebar 分层使用 depth 建立 hierarchy；主内容面板的展开/折叠只使用 transform，符合避免动画布局属性的 motion 约束。
